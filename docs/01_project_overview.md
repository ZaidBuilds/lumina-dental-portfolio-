# 📋 01 — Project Overview

---

## 🧠 What Problem Are We Solving?

Most dental clinic websites are boring, generic, and forgettable.
They look exactly like a Word document printed to a web page: white background, blue buttons, stock photos.

**This project solves that problem completely.**

We are building a **scrollytelling portfolio website** for a high-end dental clinic — one that feels like an Apple product launch page or an architectural design studio, not a GP's appointment book.

Its purpose is:
- Act as a **live portfolio** the developer shows to clients during business outreach
- To demonstrate world-class web design in the healthcare sector
- To give the dental clinic an unforgettable, premium identity online

---

## 🧑‍💼 Who Is the User?

Two types of users:

| User | Goal |
|------|------|
| **Potential dental patient** | Browse services, trust the brand, book a consultation |
| **Developer (you) showing it to clients** | Impress potential clients with your design & engineering skills |

---

## ✨ Main Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 🦷 **3D Scroll Animation** | 120-frame PNG sequence rendered on HTML5 Canvas, scrubbed by the user's scroll |
| 🌑 **Premium Dark Theme** | Jet-black `#050505` background with amber `#f59e0b` accents |
| 🖱️ **Custom Liquid Cursor** | A `mix-blend-difference` white orb that replaces the system cursor site-wide |
| 🎯 **Hero Intro Section** | Large left-aligned text with a CTA button appears on first load |
| 📜 **Scrollytelling Overlay** | Text sections ("Precision & Care", etc.) fade in over the canvas as you scroll |
| 🏆 **Animated Stats Bar** | Parallax-driven numbers: 15+ Years, 10k+ Smiles, 100% Satisfaction |
| 💆 **About the Doctor** | A full section introducing Dr. Lumina with imagery and bio text |
| 🪥 **Services Showcase** | 4-card grid for Cosmetic Dentistry, Invisalign, Implants, Whitening |
| ⭐ **Testimonials** | Patient reviews with stars to build social trust |
| 📍 **CTA + Location** | Contact info, Book Appointment button, location map card (Kuwait) |
| 🔗 **Footer with Social Links** | Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn links |

---

## 🎨 Design Vision

The design is inspired by:
- **Apple** — Clean, generous whitespace; large typography; dark aesthetics
- **Awwwards agencies** — Custom cursors, scroll-triggered animations
- **High-end medical brands** — Trust, precision, elegance

> "This is not a dental website. It is a *luxury experience* that happens to be about teeth."
